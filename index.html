The Digital Self



What I do online is me.



Wht I say online is me.



Photos of me online are me.



Videos of me online are me.



My location right now is me.



Where I am going is me.



Where I came from is me.



What I watch online is me.



What music I listen to is me.



What podcasts I listen to is me.



The events I attend are me.



My relationships with other people is me.



What I purchase online is me.



What I read on and offline is me.



The data I produce each day online is me.



I am not for sale to the highest bidder.



I am always skeptical of new applications.



I spend time to unsubscribe my email.



I delete online accounts I do not use.



I regularly cleanup my online information.



I make sure applications bring me joy.



I regularly get offline to do other things.



It is time I went for a walk.



It is time to read a book.



It is time for my family.



It is time for me.



I matter.



I matter online.



I matter offline.
